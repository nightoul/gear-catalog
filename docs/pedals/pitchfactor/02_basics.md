## THE BASICS



### MODES OF OPERATION

Eventide PitchFactor has two modes of operation: Play Mode and Bank Mode. To switch between them, press and hold the Right footswitch for 2 seconds. Footswtiches have different functions in each mode.



### BANKS AND PRESETS

The pedal has 100 preset slots organized into 50 banks (numbered 1-50). Each bank has 2 presets.

The pedal has three footswitches. When in Bank Mode, the Left and Middle footswitches correspond to a preset in a bank. A red LED indicates that the preset is active. The Right footswitch scrolls up to the next bank.

#### Factory presets

The unit ships with 100 factory presets stored from 1:1 to 50:2. The factory presets are great and can be used without any additional tweaking.

#### Cycling through presets

When in Bank Mode, step on the Right footswitch to scroll up to the next bank. When a bank is selected, the Left and Middle footswitches choose Preset 1 or Preset 2.

The Encoder knob can scroll through banks up and down, but only immediately after pressing the Right footswitch. An auxilary switch can also be programmed to bank down.

In the System Mode, banks can also be deactivated to decrease the total number of active banks.



### SAVING A PRESET

#### To save to a current location

1. Hold the Encoder knob
2. Display shows the current bank and preset numbers
3. Hold the Encoder knob again. The display shows SAVED

#### To save to a new location

1. Hold the Encoder knob
2. Display shows the current bank and preset numbers
3. Turn the Encoder knob to select bank and preset locations
4. Hold the Encoder knob again. The display shows SAVED

#### To cancel save

To cancel an in-progress save, push the Encoder knob or step on any footswitch. The display shows NO SAVE.
